# Dinner Party Leader Voiceover Script

**Duration:** ~60 seconds
**Audience:** New dinner party leaders being onboarded to Togather
**Tone:** Warm, encouraging, practical

---

## Script

Hey — welcome to Togather. You've been trusted to lead a dinner party, and this app is here to make that easy.

**Getting started takes 30 seconds.** Sign in with your phone number, enter the verification code, and you're in. Find your community and you'll see your dinner party waiting for you.

**First thing — make it yours.** Add a photo, write a short description, set your address, and pick your regular meeting day and time. This is what people see when they discover your group, so make it inviting.

**When it's time to meet, create an event.** Set the date, time, and location — members get notified automatically. They can RSVP right in the app, and you'll see exactly who's coming. After the gathering, mark attendance so you always know who showed up.

**Your members are the heart of this.** The app tracks who's been coming and who's been missing. Use follow-ups to log a call, a text, or a quick check-in — so nobody slips through the cracks. You'll see at a glance who might need a little extra care.

**Communication happens right here too.** Your group has a built-in chat — share updates, send encouragement, coordinate plans. And if your people already live on WhatsApp, Slack, or Discord, you can link your external chat so everyone knows where to find each other.

**You've also got tools most group apps don't give you** — welcome bots for new members, birthday celebrations, custom resources, and smart reminders that run on autopilot.

This is just the beginning. We're building Togather to be the best tool for leaders like you — and your feedback shapes what comes next. So jump in, host your first dinner, and let's build community together.
