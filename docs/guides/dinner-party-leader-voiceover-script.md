# Dinner Party Leader Voiceover Script (1 minute)

**Purpose:** Pastor voiceover for rollout to dinner party leaders  
**Duration:** ~1 minute (~150 words at natural pace)

---

## Script (~150 words / ~1 min)

**[Warm opening]**

Hey everyone—I'm excited to share something that's going to make leading your dinner party so much easier. We're rolling out Togather, an app built specifically for you.

**[Sign in]**

Signing in is simple. Use your phone number, get a quick verification code, and you're in.

**[Personalize]**

Once you're in, make your dinner party your own. Add a photo, update the name and description, and set your location. A few minutes and it feels like home.

**[Events & invitations]**

Events are where it gets powerful. Create your next gathering and share the link. When you send it—in WhatsApp, Slack, Discord, or a text—it shows up as a beautiful preview with the image, date, and details. People know exactly what they're signing up for.

**[Members & care]**

Your Leader Tools let you see who's in your group, track attendance, and record who came. Add guests, see RSVPs at a glance, and follow up with who needs it. It's built to help you care for your people, not just manage a list.

**[Future & close]**

We're just getting started. There's more coming—and I can't wait to see how you use this to build deeper connections around your tables. Let's go.

---

## Shorter Version (~120 words / ~50 sec)

For a tighter 1-minute read with pauses, use this trimmed version:

> Hey everyone—we're rolling out Togather, an app built for dinner party leaders. Sign in with your phone and a quick verification code. Make your dinner party your own—add a photo, name, description, and location. Create events and share the link; when you send it in WhatsApp, Slack, Discord, or a text, it shows a beautiful preview so people know what they're signing up for. Leader Tools let you see members, track attendance, add guests, and follow up with who needs it. We're just getting started—there's more coming. Let's go.

---

## Speaking Notes

- **Pace:** Speak naturally; aim for ~2.5 words per second. Pause briefly after each section.
- **Tone:** Warm, encouraging, pastoral—like you're inviting them into something good.
- **Emphasis:** Slightly emphasize "Leader Tools," "beautiful preview," and "care for your people."
- **Optional:** Add a 2–3 second pause before "Let's go" for impact.
