# Android Closed Test — Instructions for Testers

We're putting Togather on the Google Play Store. Before Google lets us publish,
we need **at least 12 people to test the app for 14 days straight**. Takes 2
minutes to join, then just keep the app installed and open it now and then.

## What you need
- An **Android phone** (this is Android-only — iPhone users already have us on the App Store).
- A **Google account** — and it must be the **same Google account that's signed
  into the Play Store on your phone**. This is the #1 thing people get wrong.

## How to join (2 minutes)
1. Tap this link on your phone: **`<OPT-IN LINK — paste from Play Console>`**
2. Tap **"Become a tester."**
3. It opens the Togather page in the Play Store — tap **Install**.
   - ⏳ It can take a few minutes (sometimes up to a couple hours) to show up.
     If it's not there yet, don't worry — check back later.
4. Open the app and sign in like normal.

## What we need from you for 14 days
- **Keep the app installed** for the full two weeks.
- **Open it a few times** over those two weeks — browse your community, check
  events, send a message. Google checks that testers actually use it, not just
  leave it sitting there.
- Tell us if anything looks broken or off.

## Troubleshooting
- **"I clicked the link but the app isn't in my Play Store."** You likely opted
  in with a different Google account than the one on your phone. Check which
  account your Play Store uses (Play Store → profile icon, top right) and opt in
  with that exact account.
- **Still nothing after a few hours?** Send us the email on your Play Store
  account and we'll double-check you're on the tester list.

Thank you 🙏 — this directly unlocks the Play Store launch for the whole community.
