What's New

Follow our journey as we build the best community app. Here's everything we've shipped.

January 2026 Latest

Threaded Conversations

Reply to specific messages in chat with Slack-style threads. Keep discussions organized with reply count indicators and a dedicated thread view for deeper conversations.

Message Reactions

Express yourself with emoji reactions on messages. Tap any reaction to see who responded, bringing more life to your group conversations.

Planning Center Integration

Connect your community to Planning Center for seamless member syncing. Import your existing member data and keep everything in sync automatically.

Automated Bots

Welcome new members automatically with customizable welcome messages. Birthday celebrations with timezone awareness. Task reminders with @mention notifications.

Community-Wide Events

Create events visible to your entire community, not just specific groups. Perfect for church-wide gatherings, community celebrations, and announcements.

Android App

Togather is now available for Android devices. Download the APK directly from our website and join your community on any device.

December 2025

@Mentions

Tag other members in messages with @username to get their attention. Mentioned users receive push notifications so they never miss important messages.

Rich Link Previews

Share Togather event and group links anywhere and see beautiful previews. Open Graph support means your links look great on social media, in texts, and beyond.

Community Theming

Custom accent colors throughout the app based on your community's branding. Make Togather feel like your own with personalized colors and styling.

Leader Tools

Track attendance patterns and identify who needs follow-up with connection scoring. See attendance rates and reach-out scores to prioritize member outreach.

RSVP from Chat

See event cards embedded in chat and RSVP directly without leaving the conversation. Fun GIF animations celebrate when you confirm attendance.

Account Management

Delete your account with SMS verification if you need to. Set your timezone for accurate event times. Leave communities when it's time to move on.

Admin Dashboard

Restructured admin page with dedicated Stats tab. Clickable stats cards link to detailed member lists. Manage pending join requests in a dedicated screen.

NearMe Discovery

Discover public groups near you without logging in. Browse by location on an interactive map and find your community before you even sign up.

Event Sharing

Share events with unique links. Non-members can RSVP with just their phone number, making it easy to invite friends and family.

Announcement Groups

Special read-only groups for community-wide announcements. Keep everyone informed without the noise of replies cluttering the channel.

November 2025

Map-Based Discovery

Interactive 3D map with Mapbox buildings for exploring groups near you. Find groups based on location and see them visualized on a beautiful map interface.

Real-Time Chat

Complete chat redesign centered on conversations. Real-time messaging with rich features powered by Stream Chat. Stay connected with your groups instantly.

Event Management

Create and manage events for your groups. Schedule one-time or recurring meetings, track RSVPs, and send reminders to attendees.

Phone Authentication

Secure login with SMS codes. Simple, secure authentication that works with any phone number. No passwords to remember.

Attendance Tracking

Leaders can track who shows up to meetings. Post-meeting prompts help confirm attendance, building a picture of engagement over time.

iOS App Launch

Togather launches on the App Store. Available for iPhone and iPad, bringing community connection to your pocket.
