# Security Policy

## Reporting a Vulnerability

If you discover a security vulnerability in Togather, please report it responsibly.

**Do NOT open a public GitHub issue for security vulnerabilities.**

Instead, please email: **togather@supa.media**

### What to Include

- Description of the vulnerability
- Steps to reproduce
- Potential impact
- Suggested fix (if any)

### What to Expect

- **Acknowledgment** within 48 hours of your report
- **Status update** within 5 business days
- **Resolution timeline** based on severity

### Severity Levels

| Severity | Response Time | Examples |
|----------|--------------|---------|
| Critical | 24 hours | Auth bypass, data exposure, RCE |
| High | 3 days | Privilege escalation, XSS |
| Medium | 7 days | CSRF, information disclosure |
| Low | 30 days | Minor issues, hardening suggestions |

## Supported Versions

| Version | Supported |
|---------|-----------|
| Latest  | Yes       |

## Responsible Disclosure

We ask that you:

1. Give us reasonable time to fix the issue before public disclosure
2. Do not access or modify other users' data
3. Do not degrade the service (DoS, spam, etc.)
4. Act in good faith

We will:

1. Acknowledge your contribution
2. Keep you informed of our progress
3. Credit you in our changelog (unless you prefer anonymity)
